Welcome to LearnSphere, our online learning platform designed to help students and professionals upgrade their skills.

Account Registration:
Users must create an account using a valid email address. Each account is intended for individual use only and should not be shared with others. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials.

Course Access:
Once enrolled in a course, users receive lifetime access to the course materials unless otherwise stated. Some courses may include downloadable resources such as PDFs, templates, or practice datasets.

Payments and Refunds:
All payments are processed securely through our payment partners. We offer a 14-day refund policy for most courses, provided that less than 30% of the course content has been completed. Refunds are not available for subscription-based plans after the billing cycle has started.

Certificates:
Certificates of completion are issued automatically after a user completes all required modules and quizzes in a course. Certificates are provided in digital format only.

Technical Requirements:
Users are responsible for ensuring they have a stable internet connection and an updated web browser. LearnSphere is not responsible for interruptions caused by user-side technical issues.

Code of Conduct:
Users must not engage in harassment, spam, or unauthorized distribution of course content. Violation of these terms may result in account suspension or permanent removal from the platform.
